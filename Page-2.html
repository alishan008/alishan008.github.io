Backorder Prediction

 A product being backordered means placing an order for a later delivery date after the product has sold out. It is a frustration experience for everyone involved in the supply chain i.e. buyers, sellers, distributors. If only there was a way to predict which products are likely to get backordered. this ML project is made with the objective of solving this problem by making this prediction for you using the product's performance forecast and supply chain variables.

Live demo available at Backorder Predictor
